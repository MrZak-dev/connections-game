How to Play

Group words that share a common thread.

search

Find Groups of Four

Identify four items that have something in common. Categories can be anything from book titles to words that start with the same letter.

check_circle

Select & Submit

Tap to select four words, then tap the 'Submit' button to lock in your guess.

warning

Four Mistakes Allowed

You can make up to four mistakes before the game ends. Keep a close eye on your remaining attempts.
