How to Play

Find groups of four words that share something in common. For example, the words below can be grouped into two categories:

APPLE
DAFFODIL
ROSE
GOOGLE
AMAZON
TULIP
META
LILY

Category Examples

Categories can be anything from types of flowers to tech companies.

FLOWER

DAFFODIL, LILY, ROSE, TULIP

BIG TECH COMPANIES

AMAZON, APPLE, GOOGLE, META

Category Difficulty

Each puzzle has four categories. The categories are color-coded by difficulty:

  • Easiest
  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard

You can make up to four mistakes before the game ends. Good luck!
